About Us

The frontend landscape is witnessing a paradigm shift because of changes in technologies, plenty of device sizes, connection speed and loads of other factors. To address these developments, we are organizing Frontend Engineers Meet.

Frontend Engineers Meet is a one-day event for the web professionals and aims to bring together the bright minds from Frontend Engineering, UI Development, HTML & CSS, Angular JS and Polymer JS domains.

The meet gives you an opportunity to learn and share your passion about User Experience and related disciplines.
