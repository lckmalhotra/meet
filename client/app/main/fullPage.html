About Us

FE CONF is a one day conference held in Noida. It is dedicated to the Front End Engineers community in India and aims at bringing together tech and design people to exchange ideas and knowledge.

The conference is an excellent platform for people from Front End Engineering, UI Development, HTML & CSS, Angular JS and Polymer JS domains to present their projects, latest research and technologies in the fields of Web technologies, Web design and UI.

Our last conference had an amazing feedback, it helped people to find new ideas, learn from brilliant people, get answers to a lot of questions, some even found new jobs and made good friends.
