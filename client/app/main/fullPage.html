About Us

Front-end design and development is a frontier in 2016 and with changes in technologies, plenty of device sizes, connection speed and loads of other factors, it becomes furthermore crucial. To address these developments, we are organizing Frontend Engineers Meet.

Frontend Engineers Meet is a one-day event for the web professionals and aims to bring together the bright minds from Frontend Engineering, UI Development, HTML & CSS, Angular JS and Polymer JS domains.

The conference will let you explore real-life web design problems and techniques that are relevant and applicable to your work straight away.
